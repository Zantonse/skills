# EDC Festival Aesthetic — Electric Sunset

**Visual identity for EDC Las Vegas 2026 VIP app icons and assets.**

---

## Core Concept

> *"Electric sky meets desert night."*

- Deep violet darkness as the canvas
- Gold and amber as the language of luxury
- Neon glow as the energy of the festival
- Minimal, iconic, instantly readable at a glance

---

## Style Direction

### What to aim for
- **Flat icon design** — Clean silhouettes, minimal detail
- **Neon glow effects** — Subtle luminous halos around key shapes
- **Dark transparent backgrounds** — Icons float on dark surfaces
- **Bold, readable shapes** — Recognizable at 32px or 512px
- **Electric Sunset palette** — Deep purple/violet with gold and coral

### What to avoid
- Hand-drawn or sketch styles
- Photorealistic rendering
- Busy compositions with many elements
- Light or white backgrounds
- Pastel or muted colors

---

## Color System

### Background
```
Primary         transparent / deep violet #1a0a2e
```

### Icon Colors
```
Gold            #FFD200   (primary accent — VIP warmth)
Amber/Coral     #FF6B6B   (secondary accent — energy)
White/Pale Gold #FFF5CC   (highlight/glow center)
Deep Violet     #2d1b4e   (shadow/depth)
```

### Color Usage
- **Gold** dominates each icon (60-70%)
- **Amber/coral** for secondary elements or accents (15-20%)
- **Pale gold white** for glows and highlights (10%)
- **Background** — transparent or deep violet
